West Bengal Recovery Framework

A comprehensive research and policy framework for land rights recovery, property restitution, wetland restoration, welfare integrity, and transparent governance in West Bengal.








Overview

The West Bengal Recovery Framework is an independent research initiative that explores evidence-based approaches to addressing challenges related to:

Property restitution and dispute resolution
Land rights documentation and reform
Bheri wetland conservation and restoration
Operation Barga historical analysis
Welfare scheme transparency and beneficiary verification
GIS-powered land intelligence systems
AI-assisted document forensics
Blockchain-based land registry concepts
Open governance and public accountability

This repository presents a structured framework for policymakers, researchers, legal scholars, technologists, environmental experts, and governance practitioners interested in transparent and constitutional approaches to resource recovery and institutional reform.
